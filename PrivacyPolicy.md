# Privacy Policy for Whispy

**Last Updated:** March 16, 2026

Mallows ("the Bot") is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of its users. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

## 1. Data Collection
We collect minimal data necessary for the Bot's functionality:
* **User IDs:** Stored for moderation (blacklisting) and AFK systems.
* **Server IDs:** Stored to save configuration settings (e.g., confession channel IDs).
* **Message Content:** Confessions are processed to be posted anonymously. Depending on server settings, logs may be temporarily cached to facilitate the "Reply" and "Review" systems.

## 2. Anonymity Guarantee
The core mission of Mallows is anonymity. 
* We do not reveal the identity of a "confessor" to server staff or other users.
* Metadata linked to confessions is only accessible to Bot Developers in cases of extreme Terms of Service violations (e.g., illegal activity).

## 3. Data Retention & Deletion
* **Configuration Data:** Deleted automatically if the Bot leaves a server.
* **User Data:** Users may request a manual data wipe by contacting the development team through the [Official Support Server].

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Data is only shared with Discord's API as required for the Bot to operate.
